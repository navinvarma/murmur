Before you start recording, here's how Murmur handles your data.

What stays on your device

Audio stays in your browser

Recordings are stored in memory only — never uploaded. They are lost when you close or reload the page.

Notes & transcripts saved locally

Titles and transcript text are saved in your browser's localStorage — on your device only, never sent anywhere.

No accounts, tracking, or ads

Murmur is fully client-side. No sign-ups, no analytics, no cookies, no third-party scripts — except for the transcription service below.

Transcription

Live transcription sends audio to Google

This page uses the Web Speech API, which routes your voice through Google's servers in Chrome and Edge. Google's Privacy Policy applies. Avoid sensitive information if this is a concern.

Prefer fully local transcription?

Try Murmur AI — it runs Whisper entirely in your browser using WebGPU. Nothing leaves your device.

By continuing you acknowledge that live transcription on this page sends audio to Google for processing. For fully private transcription, use Murmur AI instead.
